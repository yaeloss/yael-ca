The Consumer Choice Center went to Luxembourg to provide policymakers, civil society leaders, religious groups, and labor unions with lessons on the legalization of cannabis from Canada. The CCC promoted the idea of "smart" legalization that would embrace consumer access, competition, safety, and eradication of the black market. This report aired on Luxembourg's RTL station on the evening news.
