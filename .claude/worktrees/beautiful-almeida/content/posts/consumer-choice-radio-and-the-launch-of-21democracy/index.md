Today the Consumer Choice Center today announced its new radio program, Consumer Choice Radio, to be broadcast from 10:00-11:00 AM on Saturdays on The Big Talker 106.7 FM out of Wilmington, NC.

Thanks to a partnership with the radio station and lead talent Joe Catenacci, Consumer Choice Radio will bring the best news, analysis, and updates from the global movement for consumer choice. Featuring activists, entrepreneurs, and newsmakers of all types,

Added to that, we've also launched the 21Democracy project, focused on countering the influence of authoritarian regimes and making the case for liberal democracies. Find out more at [21democracy.com](http://21democracy.com)
