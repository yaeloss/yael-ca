Starting next week, Consumer Choice Radio will be syndicated on its second affiliate: Sauga 960 AM, broadcasting to over 4 million listeners in the Greater Toronto Area, Ontario.

You'll hear Yaël Ossowski and David Clement Thursdays at 1PM EST, and on The Big Talker 106.7 FM Saturdays at 10AM EST.

We're now an internationally syndicated radio show!

Be sure to subscribe to the podcast version delivered each week if you're outside of radio range.
