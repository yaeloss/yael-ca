By Yaël Ossowski | Florida Watchdog

> TAMPA — The congressman with a knack for colorful hyperbole and an audible distaste for Republicans is back
> 
> After losing District 8 to Republican Daniel Webster in the 2010 midterm elections, former Democratic U.S. Rep. [**Alan Grayson**](http://www.congressmanwithguts.com/) now is aiming for the newly configured and Democratic-friendly 9th Congressional District, south of **Orlando**.
> 
> Grayson first came to prominence in the U.S. House floor debates over health-care reform in September 2009. He summarized the Republicans’ plan for health care as “[**don’t get sick, and if you do get sick, die quickly**](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=bSRo51SbQQs),” drawing ire from many of his GOP colleagues.
> 
> He stoked further controversy when he described the nation’s current health-care regime as a “[**Holocaust in America**](http://www.jta.org/news/article/2009/10/06/1008327/grayson-regrets-use-of-holocaust),” and described those opposed to **President****Barack Obama**‘s proposed reform as “[**foot-dragging, knuckle-dragging Neanderthals**](http://politicalticker.blogs.cnn.com/2009/09/30/grayson-calls-republicans-knuckle-dragging-neanderthals/).”
> 
> But his most adversarial language may have occurred throughout his 2010 campaign for re-election against former Florida House Speaker **[Webster](http://ballotpedia.org/wiki/index.php/Daniel_Webster_%28Florida%29).**
> 
> In a widely circulated ad, he labeled Webster “**Taliban Dan**” for certain fundamentalist comments about religion, as well as accused him of dodging the **Vietnam War** draft by receiving medical deferments, actions which cost Grayson votes and earned him the label of America’s “[**worst politician**](http://www.thedailybeast.com/newsweek/2010/10/24/america-s-worst-politician.html)” by conservative columnist **George Will**.
