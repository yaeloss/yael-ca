By Yaël Ossowski | Florida Watchdog

> ORLANDO — Frustrated with accounts of voter fraud and ineligible voters statewide, residents of **Orange County** undertook their own investigation to reveal whether noncitizens were present on the county voter rolls.
> 
> **Kelli McNair-Lee** and **Bill Barnett**, members of the **East Side Tea Party** and**Eastern Orlando Tea Party****,**petitioned **Orange County Jury Services** for the list of people exempt from jury duty, because they were not citizens.
