By Yaël Ossowski | Florida Watchdog

> TAMPA — In the search to find the biggest job creator in **Florida**, residents should look no further than themselves — or rather, their government.

> The single largest employer in 51 of 67 Florida counties is the public sector, including members of the military, librarians, college professors, prison guards, administrators and health-care workers, according to [an examination](http://www.floridataxwatch.org/resources/pdf/jobsbriefing.pdf) by **Florida TaxWatch**, a nonprofit fiscal watchdog in **Tallahassee**.
