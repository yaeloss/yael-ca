This 01. March 2013 show was broadcast from **die Freiheit Studio** in Vienna, Austria on the [Liberty Radio Network](http://lrn.fm) and the [No Agenda Stream](http://noagendastream.com).

My guest this hour was **David Lopez** of [**Australians For Liberty**](http://www.australiansforliberty.org/).

Thanks to David’s analysis, we get the low-down on exactly how political corruption and imperial aggression are working in tandem to limit freedoms down under in the great country and continent of Australia.

As well as learning about David’s [new site and project](http://facebook.com/australiansforliberty), we talked about the government cracking down on knives, thug-like checkpoints and shakedowns, the predatory nature of central banks, how Australia pimps the USA’s “terror threats,” censoring the internet, hopes for freedom, moving beyond government roads, and much more.
