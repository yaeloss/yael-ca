By Yaël Ossowski | Florida Watchdog

SANFORD — In more tranquil times, the city of **Sanford** was known as the **Historic Waterfront Gateway City**, a tribute to its comfortable position between Lakes **Monroe** and **Jesup**.

But, since the death of **Trayvon Martin**in late February, the city has become the volatile epicenter of the battle for justice, social activism and political maneuvering leading up to the fall elections.
