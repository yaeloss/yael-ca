By Yaël Ossowski | Florida Watchdog

> ST. PETERSBURG— The comfortable room had patriotic flare and subdued excitement.
> 
> The [national debt clock](http://www.usdebtclock.org/) stood ticking behind 22 hand-picked senior citizens armed with smartphones, tablets and buttons with U.S. flags.
> 
> They were among the 300 supporters of Republican presidential nominee **Mitt Romney** gathered Wednesday morning at the **Mirror Lake Lyceum** downtown.
