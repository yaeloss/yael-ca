> _**“Tuscan Love”**_
> 
> _Directed by Yaël Ossowski_
> 
> _Freiheit Productions_

My documentary on an excursion to celebrate love in the Tuscan hills of Italy.

Discover the rustic towns of **Vinci** and **Monsummano Terme** to uncover the beautiful landscapes and historic villas. Hitch a seat at a traditional Tuscan wedding in **Montecatini**, enjoy the blissful poetry of [**Dante**](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dante_Alighieri), and explore the romantic streets of **Montalcino** and **Florence**.

Running time: (17:29)

I shot all the video on an iPhone 4S and edited on an open-source Linux version of **Kdenlive**. All music acquired freely and without license at [freeplaymusic.com](http://freeplaymusic.com/).
