Consumer Choice Center Deputy Director and Bitcoin Policy Institute Fellow Yaël Ossowski presents the"Congressional Guide to Bitcoin" at the 2025 Bitcoin Policy Summit.

This includes reforms for De Minimis Taxation Exemptions, reforms of the Bank Secrecy Act, and changes to the Fannie Mae Selling Guide to allow Bitcoin and its crypto-offspring to be used as collateral for mortgages and home financing.
