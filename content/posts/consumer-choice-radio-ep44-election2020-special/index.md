For this #Election2020 special, we check in with some of our Consumer Choice Center colleagues to get their take on some of the results (still pending) from the U.S. elections. We cover the Biden vs. Trump race, but also the ballot propositions that are awesome for #consumerchoice, various scenarios for the House and Senate, and what all this will mean for our work at the Consumer Choice Center.

Joining us for election breakdown:

- Fred Roeder: Managing Director
- Bill Wirtz: Senior Policy Analyst
- Fabio Fernandes: Media Associate
