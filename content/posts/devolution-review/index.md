The launch of [Devolution Review](http://DevolutionReview.com).

## Devolution Review is a quarterly literary journal that features book reviews, short stories, nonfiction, poetry, personal narratives, interviews, and more.

## Founded in Vienna, Austria in February 2017, the journal boasts editors and contributors around the world.

## Our mission is to tell the stories of modern cosmopolitanism through the written word.

## The vision of Devolution Review is a critical and skeptical readership who recognize the beauty of all that binds us.
