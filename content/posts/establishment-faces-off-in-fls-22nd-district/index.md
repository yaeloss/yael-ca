By Yaël Ossowski | Florida Watchdog  

> MIAMI — More than four months remain until the primaries for the Republican and Democratic parties in **Florida**, but the parties have decided on their candidates for the 22nd Congressional District.

> In the race to replace GOP **U.S. Rep.** [**Allen West**](http://ballotpedia.org/wiki/index.php/Allen_West), who is seeking election in the newly drawn 18th District, the establishments of both parties have set up former state**Rep.** **[Adam Hasner](http://ballotpedia.org/wiki/index.php/Adam_Hasner)**, R-**Boca Raton**, and former **West Palm Beach** **Mayor** **[Lois Frankel](http://ballotpedia.org/wiki/index.php/Lois_Frankel)**, the Democrat, as competitors for the November election.
