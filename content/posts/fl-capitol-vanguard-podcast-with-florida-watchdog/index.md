By Yaël Ossowski | Florida Watchdog

> TALLAHASSEE— On Aug. 7, [**Florida Watchdog**](http://floridawatchdog.org/) Bureau Chief **Yaël Ossowski**, joined [**Capitol Vanguard**](http://capitolvanguard.org/) reporter **Will Patrick** for a discussion about Florida’s top news stories.
> 
> Topics covered in the podcast include attempts to overcome voter fraud, the growing absentee ballot scandal in Miami-Dade County, politicians protesting military sequestration cuts, and the newly upheld health care reform known as ‘Obamacare’ and its affect on Florida residents.
