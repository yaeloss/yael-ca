By  Yaël Ossowski and Marianela Toledo | Florida Watchdog

> PEMBROKE PINES — Speaking [exclusively](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=OjXDIkHwfB8&feature=plcp) to **Florida Watchdog**, businesswoman **[Karen Harrington](http://www.google.com/url?sa=t&rct=j&q=&esrc=s&source=web&cd=1&ved=0CF4QFjAA&url=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.karenforcongress.com%2F&ei=xEXbT-e_BoGo8QSetvzTCg&usg=AFQjCNEnfpdbCNtHPGzeAjXIZ0Dnxse8kg&sig2=_zAnE87ZNhPqZ9l1WkmqOg)** laid out her case for the Republican nomination in the battle for the Congressional District 23 seat against Democratic U.S. Rep. **[Debbie Wasserman Schultz](http://ballotpedia.com/wiki/index.php/Debbie_Wasserman_Schultz)**.
> 
> “The first  thing we have to do is not spend more money than we take in,” she told Florida Watchdog.
> 
> “For the last four years, we have national debts ranging over $1 trillion each year, and that’s what is continuing to contribute to our national debt.
> 
> “So we have to get our spending under control, just like you and I would do in business and in our personal lives,” said Harrington, owner of **Rickey’s Restaurant and Lounge** in **Hollywood**and **Pembroke Pines**.
