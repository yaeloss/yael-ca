On Wednesday, I had the privilege of hosting the morning radio program on [Big Talker 106.7FM](http://bigtalkerfm.com), Mornings with Joe Catenacci, while Joe took a much-needed break.

You can find the entire program in [audio format](http://yael.ca/wp-content/uploads/2021/06/16junemjc.mp3) here or on [Joe's website](https://joecatenacci.com/wednesday-june-16th-2021/), and in video form in full here with some clips peppered on my own [YouTube page](https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCZw8x8wYVvvR1ZC-Szgj1Gw). The radio station's [YouTube stream](https://youtu.be/WWfSpWllQ9A?t=715) version is below.

https://youtu.be/WWfSpWllQ9A?t=715

My guests were [U.S. Rep. Dan Bishop](https://danbishop.house.gov/), Mark Marcley of [Recovery Hope Radio](http://recoveringhope.org), [Lillian Beecroft](http://twitter.com/lillianbeecroft) of [Awaken to Greatness](https://www.facebook.com/awakentogreatness), [Ashley Baker](http://twitter.com/andashleysays) of the [Committee For Justice](https://www.committeeforjustice.org/) and the [Alliance on Antitrust](https://www.allianceonantitrust.org/), and [David Clement](http://davidclement.ca), my co-host on [Consumer Choice Radio](http://consumerchoiceradio.com) and colleague at the [Consumer Choice Center](http://consumerchoicecenter.org).
