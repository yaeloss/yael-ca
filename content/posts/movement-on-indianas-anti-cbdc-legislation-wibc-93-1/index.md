I joined Stephen Kent on WIBC 93.1 to break down the case for a bill blocking the use of CBDC's in Indiana. Sen. Eric Koch introduced Senate Bill 180 (SB180) to prohibit state agencies from accepting payments made with a central bank digital currency for any service, tax, license, permit, fee, information, or other amount due the governmental body. It would also bar government agencies from requiring payments to be made with a central bank digital currency. This is good for consumers in Indiana.
