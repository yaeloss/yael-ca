There was a [grand debate](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=m79G0ZxLjxg) at **Channel 6 Studios** in Trenton, N.J., on Friday to determine who would be the best candidate for U.S. Senate.

There was this guy, Cory Booker, the Democratic candidate and establishment favorite:

![bookerintro](images/bookerintro.gif)

And his opponent, the Republican conservative activist who [gave Gov. Chris Christie a run for his money](http://www.nj.com/news/index.ssf/2009/06/chris_christie_defeats_steve_l.html) in the 2009 gubernatorial primary:

![loneganintro](images/loneganintro1.gif)

Steve Lonegan pleaded for a more time to implement the controversial law.
