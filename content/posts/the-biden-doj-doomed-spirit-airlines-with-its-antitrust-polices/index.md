Consumer Choice Center Deputy Director Yaël Ossowski is interviewed on Schwab Network to discuss the looming bankruptcy of Spirit Airlines, and how the Biden administration's antitrust and competition policies have precipitated its downfall.

If we want more airline competition, we should look to repealing cabotage laws and boosting the ability for global airlines to serve US domestic travelers.
