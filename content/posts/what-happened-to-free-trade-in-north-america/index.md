Even though the United States-Mexico-Canada Free Trade Agreement has been completed and is ready to be ratified, it hasn't moved through Congress. What's the holdup? And why is no Democratic presidential candidate backing free trade as a principle? CCC Deputy Director Yaël Ossowski breaks it down.

Broadcast on the Joe Catenacci Show on The Big Talker 106.7FM on November 15, 2019.
